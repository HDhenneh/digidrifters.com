⚠ Russia's formal constitutional structure mirrors a conventional presidential republic. It is not. The chart below shows both the legal architecture (largely decorative) and the actual power structure — the "vertical of power" (вертикаль власти) — which operates through personal loyalty, coercion, and patronage rather than institutions.

VLADIMIR PUTIN
